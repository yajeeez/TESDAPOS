How to Use the TESDA POS Food Ordering System

Follow these simple steps to place your order easily!

Step 1: Register / Log In

Create a new account or log in with your existing credentials to start ordering.

Step 2: Browse Menu

Explore our wide selection of delicious meals and beverages.

Step 3: Add to Cart

Select your desired meals and add them to your cart.

Step 4: Checkout

Review your order, confirm details, and proceed to checkout.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Pick up your food and enjoy your meal. Thank you for ordering with us!
